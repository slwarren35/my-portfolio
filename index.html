About Me

Sheri Warren's Journey: I am a former high school math teacher. Upon graduation from college, I entered the teaching profession. I taught for three years and then stayed at home to raise our children. As my children got older, I worked part-time as an Office Manager for a local dance studio and volunteered my time on our local Amelia Day Committee.

It was during my time as an Office Manager at the local studio that I was introduced to maintaining webpages. I had to teach myself some basic HTML to update our studio webpage. Then, as that page became obsolete I used a graphic interface to completely design a brand new webpage. I was intrigued then and wanted to learn how to code it from scratch.

I returned to teaching as my kids got older and the first year back I was tasked with teaching our Computer Math class. I had the opportunity to teach students basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I had to teach myself the basics before teaching my students. This experiece reignited my desire to become a web devloper.

In 2021, I entered the University of Richmond Web Development Boot Camp to help me reach my goal of becoming a web developer. I am so excited to begin my new career.
